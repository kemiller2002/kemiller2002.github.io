How I Work

Most failures are not caused by a lack of effort. They happen when teams commit to a story before they understand the system they are acting on. I default to evidence first, reversible moves when possible, and precise language when ambiguity is hiding risk.

That approach has been shaped by work in software delivery, security-sensitive systems, compliance, diagnostics, and high-friction legacy environments where mistakes compound quickly.
