Speaker Bio

Kevin Miller is an engineering leader, systems diagnostician, and advisor with more than 25 years of experience helping organizations understand why complex systems fail and how to make better decisions under constraint. His work combines technical depth, pattern recognition, and a practical bias toward evidence over theater.

He has led engineering teams across financial, nonprofit, public-sector, and software product environments; redesigned multi-million-dollar platforms; and built products and advisory practices around modernization, diagnostics, and evidence-heavy workflows. His recent work spans AI adoption, delivery architecture, compliance-minded documentation, security-sensitive systems, and technical forensics.

A former Microsoft MVP, published speaker, and trained chocolatier, Kevin brings a blend of technical rigor, clear storytelling, and unusual cross-domain perspective. His talks help leaders and engineers reason more clearly about software, systems, risk, and the human factors that shape all of them.
