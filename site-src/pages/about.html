About

Kevin M. Miller is an engineering executive, systems strategist, and founder of Echelon Foundry. He helps organizations navigate complex technical decisions by turning ambiguity into clear, defensible action.

Over more than 25 years, he has led engineering organizations, modernized enterprise platforms, built cloud and AI systems, and guided teams through modernization, compliance, security, and operational transformation. His work spans software architecture, organizational design, technical due diligence, and evidence-based decision making.

Kevin is known for approaching difficult problems diagnostically rather than ideologically. He prioritizes evidence over assumptions, measurable outcomes over technical theater, and reversible decisions whenever uncertainty is high. That perspective has been shaped by years of working in environments where legacy systems, competing incentives, and organizational complexity often obscure the real problem.

Today, his work is expressed through Echelon Foundry, the HelixNote evidence framework, and the Decision Posture methodology. Together, they help executives, investors, and engineering organizations make better technical decisions, document critical reasoning, and create systems that remain understandable long after the original decisions have been made.

Whether advising leadership, building diagnostic frameworks, or writing about engineering judgment, Kevin’s focus remains the same: helping people think more clearly about complex systems before complexity becomes crisis.
